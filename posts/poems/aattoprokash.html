Aattoprokash

in Bengali

Jotoi ba hok opor opor kotha

PhD jibon er shukh, byatha

Agaam porber onishchoyota

Doinondin jiboner kaanna gaathaa

Shob majhe theek uki mare

Shei proshonota

Kenoi ba na Hobe Bol?

Prashongik Koutuhol

Ek kaale toh chhilam bondhu jugol

Ek benchi te boshe

Journal-e te jibon koshe

Purno hoto moner shunyotaa

Bidesh babu, Aar ki korish baari phire?

Shoptaher shesh ta ki jaaye Dheere Dheere?

Raanna bannaa korish kotota?

Tui je aachish aager moton

Jaante chaash, abar tor bhoyo kore

Ekta chhowaye golap phuler

Paapri r moto jodi jhore pore

Eto kaaler alga bondhutto

Tai aral theke unki maare proshnota

Amar hoyechhe ektu bodol

Aager nirbhoy khola monta

Dyakhaye chehaaraa nokol

Tai, Theek jokhon dekhi phaanke

Beeriye aasha proshnotake

Eriye jai, jeno dekhiie nai

Dei dohai

Boro Hobar bastobikota

Kintu moner kone

Royechhe shey baalok songopone

Shudu boro hobar pholey

Lekhe shey kobitar chholey

Jaa mukhe bolar nei jo

Shei proshner uttor shomuho

Jutlo keu? Porli ki preme?

Naa ghurchish maathe ghaate?

Baari phirey, hoy ki alaap

Kono bishesh manusher Sathe?

Shoptaher shesher shaadher ranna

Porey ki taar paate?

Kothaye je shuru kori?

Ki kore je boli toke?

Etodin Jaa rekhechhi chepe

Ki ba laabh aar dhore rekhe?

Ki ba laabh aar diye chhere?

Bolte paari, jodi korish na Kichu mone

Oi je baalok kaaler din gulo

Ure eshe notun baashay

Notun bondhu paawar ashaaye

Bhorey thaakto Mone praane

Bujhini tokhon tar ki maane

Kotha Kar Ek odbhut taane

Mone hoto shurey gaane

Shudhu likhi-gaai tor naam

Shoriye rekhe shob jorota

Ashte ashte bochhor gelo

Bidyaloy sheshe bishyobidyaloy elo

Raakhlam paa chetonar jogote

Chokh mele dekhi

Byatikrom tai hoyechhe shabhabik

Nijeke jaana, chena, khonja, pawa

Etei gelo baaki bochhor kota

Poth-ta motei shoja noy

Kaar poth e ba soja hoy?

Tao premer Kotha bolle

Bhabi, laabh ki aache Korle?

Eto Baar Toh giye Elam phire

Bondho dorjay kora nere

Ebaar pothik klanto hoye

Dhoirjer pothe poreche roye

Korleo baa khanik cheshta

Kopaler daye obosheshta

Haal chharini tobu

Jodi mele shey kobhu

Gopon e kori aasha

Paabo phire karo bhalobasha

Nijeke jene o janiye

Jodi shobe ney maaniye

Khawabo byanjon

Lotabo shokole baataasha

Laaglo kemon shotti shune?

Porlo ki purono din mone?

Jaa chilaam shob

Tai aachi na aaj, gobhir Kone?

Shawrob nirobota bhalo bhalo

Boichitrotai toh jogoter aalo

Gyapon Kori shobar sheshe

Shonar jonno krytoggyotaa
